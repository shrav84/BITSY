A Podcast is something you create similar a YouTube video, thus it is totally in your control how you tread in that territory. But keep one thing in mund that as long as your podcasts are either informative, entertaintaining, relative and honest they are going to be LOVED! Unlike radio shows, you can listen to podcasts whenever and wherever you want.
